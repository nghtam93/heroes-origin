About
Origin

Heroes Origin is a series of horizontal multi-player role-playing games (MMORPG roll-screen), a unique series of games with a large loyal community globally in general, as well as in Vietnam in particular. This is the latest product to join Metaverse, developed from the famous Ninja School product line in Vietnam from 2010 to present.

Heroes Origin is a story about heroes representing the lands on their journey against evil, the invasion of evil to protect their land.
